Creamy Chicken Masala

Creamy Chicken Masala is a rich and flavorful dish that combines tender chicken pieces with a luscious, spiced cream sauce. Perfectly balanced with a blend of aromatic spices, tomatoes, and cream, this dish is sure to impress at any dinner table. Serve it with steamed rice or naan for a comforting meal that brings the essence of Indian cuisine to your home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
c

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Stir in minced garlic and ginger; cook for an additional minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in the pureed tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add garam masala, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the chicken in spices.
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until the sauce is creamy and thickened.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
